Privacy Policy

WindLimit · Last updated July 3, 2026

The short version

WindLimit does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and stores your settings only on your device.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences (home airport, aircraft, limits, theme) are saved locally on your device and never transmitted to us.

Weather data

When you select an airport, the app requests current METAR weather from aviationweather.gov (a U.S. government service). Only the airport identifier is sent; no personal information is included in these requests.

Advertising

The free version of WindLimit shows banner ads served by Google AdMob. Ad serving may involve the collection of device identifiers and coarse usage data by Google as described in the Google Privacy Policy. Purchasing the ad-free upgrade removes all advertising and its associated data collection.

Purchases

The ad-free upgrade is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Not for operational use

WindLimit is an informational tool. It is not a substitute for official weather sources, aircraft documentation, or your operator's procedures.
